# Trust and Ethics

## Positioning

The 20 Corridors is a reflective personality game, not a clinical assessment.

## Required disclaimer

English:

> The 20 Corridors is a reflective personality game. It analyzes symbolic choices and repeated decision patterns. It is not a clinical, diagnostic, or scientifically validated psychological assessment.

Arabic/Tunisian:

> الممرّات العشرين لعبة تحليل رمزية وتأملية. تحلل الاختيارات المتكررة وطريقة التعامل مع الغموض والخوف والعلاقات والدافع الداخلي. ليست اختبارًا نفسيًا طبيًا أو تشخيصًا علميًا.

## Forbidden claims

Do not claim that the app:

- diagnoses personality disorders
- detects trauma
- measures intelligence
- predicts mental illness
- provides therapy
- produces scientifically validated results
- knows the user's true identity
- reveals hidden truth with certainty

## Required wording discipline

Prefer:

- suggests
- indicates
- pattern
- tendency
- weaker if
- repeated choice signal
- symbolic decision pattern

Avoid:

- proves
- diagnoses
- clinically confirms
- you definitely are
- your disorder is
- your pathology is

## Data minimization

MVP should not collect:

- name
- age
- gender
- exact location
- health information
- personal identity attributes

MVP may collect anonymously:

- answer sequence
- generated result
- feedback rating
- optional free-text feedback
- event telemetry after consent / privacy notice

## Falsifier rule

Every full report must contain a "disproven if" or "weaker if" section.

Example:

> This reading is weaker if you chose answers mainly because they sounded aesthetic rather than because they reflected your instinctive behavior.
