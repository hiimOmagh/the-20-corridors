# Archetypes

Archetypes are derived from score patterns, not single choices.

## 1. The Observer Strategist

Detection:

```text
High OBS + ANA + CTRL
Secondary: moderate SAF, low/moderate ACT
```

Summary:

> Reduces uncertainty by reading the environment before acting.

Strength:

> Pattern recognition, timing, strategic patience.

Failure mode:

> Over-observation, delayed execution.

Disproven if:

> Direct action and risk tags are stronger than observation/control.

## 2. The Controlled Explorer

Detection:

```text
High EXP + high ANA/OBS/CTRL
Secondary: moderate SAF
```

Summary:

> Wants discovery, but prefers to enter the unknown with an information advantage.

Strength:

> Curious but not reckless.

Failure mode:

> Can over-prepare before acting.

Disproven if:

> Exploration is high but safety/control is low; then it becomes Direct Initiator.

## 3. The Direct Initiator

Detection:

```text
High ACT + RISK + LEAD/EXP
Secondary: low WAIT, low AVD
```

Summary:

> Moves toward uncertainty directly and learns through action.

Strength:

> Momentum, courage, fast response.

Failure mode:

> Under-analysis, avoidable exposure.

Disproven if:

> Observation and safety dominate direct action.

## 4. The Solitary Architect

Detection:

```text
High IND + CTRL + PRAC/ANA
Secondary: low/moderate SOC
```

Summary:

> Builds structure around autonomy and prefers self-directed environments.

Strength:

> Independence, system-building, low dependency.

Failure mode:

> Emotional distance, over-isolation.

Disproven if:

> Social/cooperative tags are stronger than independence.

## 5. The Symbolic Seeker

Detection:

```text
High INT + MEAN + ANA/OBS
Secondary: moderate EXP
```

Summary:

> Searches for hidden layers, meaning, mystery, and symbolic depth.

Strength:

> Interpretation, depth, imaginative pattern detection.

Failure mode:

> Can over-read signals or prefer meaning over action.

Disproven if:

> Practicality/action tags dominate meaning/intuition.

## 6. The Social Navigator

Detection:

```text
High SOC + REC/COOP/OBS
Secondary: moderate ANA
```

Summary:

> Understands the world through people, reactions, recognition, and group dynamics.

Strength:

> Social reading, relational intelligence, influence.

Failure mode:

> May depend too much on external response or group atmosphere.

Disproven if:

> Independence and avoidance dominate social engagement.

## 7. The Stability Guardian

Detection:

```text
High SAF + RESP + CTRL/CALM
Secondary: low RISK
```

Summary:

> Prioritizes stability, protection, predictability, and reduced exposure.

Strength:

> Reliability, duty, risk management.

Failure mode:

> Over-caution, missed opportunity.

Disproven if:

> Risk/exploration repeatedly overrides safety.

## 8. The Power Analyst

Detection:

```text
High CTRL + ANA + LEAD/REC/OBS
Core support: Q19C/D or Q20B
```

Summary:

> Seeks leverage through knowledge, timing, prediction, and control.

Strength:

> Strategy, influence, planning depth.

Failure mode:

> Can become too controlling or status-sensitive.

Disproven if:

> Love/belonging, calm, or avoidance motives dominate power/control.
